== Description ==
 
Toggly Feature Management is a powerful and easy-to-use WordPress plugin that lets you A/B test and show/hide anything on your website using feature flags. 
With Toggly, you can quickly and easily turn on and off any part or section of your website, allowing you to experiment and optimize your website's content and functionality.

Using Toggly, you can create and manage feature flags for any element on your website, including pages, posts, widgets, and more. You can then control which users can see each feature, allowing you to test new features with a select group of users before rolling them out to your entire audience.

Toggly also lets you track the performance of your features, giving you valuable insights into how each feature is performing and helping you make informed decisions about your website's content and functionality. With Toggly, you can easily experiment and optimize your website to provide the best possible experience for your users.

Try Toggly today and take control of your website's features and functionality!

== Installation ==
 
1. Upload the plugin folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ folder.
1. Go to the **Plugins** page and activate the plugin.
